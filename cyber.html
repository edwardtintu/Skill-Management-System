Roadmap to Becoming a Cybersecurity Analyst

  • Step 1: Learn IT Basics - Networking, operating systems, and system administration.
  • Step 2: Understand Security Fundamentals - Explore concepts like confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
  • Step 3: Learn Threats and Vulnerabilities - Study malware, phishing, and system vulnerabilities.
  • Step 4: Master Network Security - Firewalls, VPNs, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Step 5: Learn Ethical Hacking - Get started with penetration testing tools like Kali Linux.
  • Step 6: Dive into Security Tools - SIEM tools, vulnerability scanners, and antivirus software.
  • Step 7: Learn Incident Response - Handling breaches and forensic investigation.
  • Step 8: Master Compliance - Study frameworks like GDPR, NIST, and ISO 27001.
  • Step 9: Earn Certifications - CompTIA Security+, CEH, CISSP, etc.
  • Step 10: Build Projects - Set up home labs to simulate and solve security challenges.
  • Step 11: Job Search - Apply for cybersecurity roles and internships.
